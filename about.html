About Us

Restaurant   Overview

DEPRESSO COFFEE, owned by Ms. Dianne Kaye Niña, started way back March 2021 in Wawandue, Subic, Zambales. Step into a cozy corner of comfort, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets warm smiles and relaxed vibes. Whether you're here for your morning pick-me-up, a midday escape, or an evening catch-up with friends, we’ve got the perfect cup waiting for you. From rich espressos to creamy lattes and handcrafted pastries, every sip and bite is made with care. Come in, unwind, and make yourself at home.
